When you create your Node.js app in cPanel, it will assign a port number.
You MUST update this port in .htaccess file.

How to find your port:
1. Go to cPanel ‚Üí Setup Node.js App
2. Look for your app in the list
3. Note the port number (example: 3000, 3500, 8080, etc.)

Then edit .htaccess and replace BOTH occurrences of 3000:

Line 4: ws://127.0.0.1:3000/$1    ‚Üê Change 3000 to your port
Line 9: http://127.0.0.1:3000/$1  ‚Üê Change 3000 to your port

Example: If cPanel assigns port 3500:
Line 4: ws://127.0.0.1:3500/$1
Line 9: http://127.0.0.1:3500/$1

- 3000
- 3500

After updating, save the file and restart your Node.js app!
